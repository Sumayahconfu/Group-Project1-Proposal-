Recipe Overview

This delicious chicken and egg stew from Ethiopia and Eritrea is sure to impress even the harshest of food critics!

  • Juice from 2 lemons

Preparation Instructions

  1. Clean the raw chicken with vinegar or lemon juice in a large bowl. Massage for 30-60 seconds, rinse, and repeat with plain water. Pierce the chicken with a knife, sprinkle with salt, and let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook onions (without oil) over medium-low heat, stirring until caramelized (about 1 hour).
  3. Add oil to the onions and sautÃ© for 15-20 minutes until they darken.
  4. Mix in berbere, garlic, ginger, cardamom, and ghee with the onions.
  5. Add the chicken, stir, cover, and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Pour in 1 cup of water, cover, and cook for another 30 minutes.
  7. Boil eggs for 10 minutes, then cool in an ice bath.
  8. Peel, cut, and add eggs to the chicken stew.
  9. Season with mekelesha and salt to taste. Serve with injera, rice, or naan. Enjoy!
