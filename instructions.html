Cilantro Orange Chicken Instructions

Instructions:

  • If youâ€™re using wood skewers, start soaking them!
  • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  • Turn on the broiler, and make sure one of the oven racks is way up at the top, about 6 inches from the heating element.
  • Place chicken in a large mixing bowl.
  • Combine the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  • Pour only half of the sauce over the chicken and toss to coat; reserve the other half of the sauce for later.
  • Thread the chicken pieces onto skewers, or add the chicken directly to your sheet pan.
  • Season the chicken with a little pinch of extra salt if you like.
  • Broil for 12 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through and has a burnished and golden exterior.
  • Optional: mix a little of the reserved sauce with diced pineapple/mango/avocado for a salsa.
  • Brush the golden-brown chicken with reserved sauce.
  • Serve the chicken with rice, beans, and your fruit salsa!
  • Fresh, juicy, savory, and delicious!
