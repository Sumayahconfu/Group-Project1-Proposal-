Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish made with fettuccine, tender chicken breast, and a rich Parmesan sauce. Perfect for a quick yet satisfying meal.

Ingredients Checklist

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 8 oz Fettuccine Pasta
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Garlic (optional)

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 600 kcal
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Sodium: 150mg

How to Make Chicken Alfredo

  1. Boil water and cook the pasta until al dente.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper, and cook until golden brown.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet and add garlic (optional).
  4. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until the sauce thickens.
  5. Slice the chicken and add to the sauce with the pasta.
  6. Mix well and serve hot, topped with extra Parmesan.
